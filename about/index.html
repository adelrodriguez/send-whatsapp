A better way to send messages. How does it work?

Welcome to sendawhats.app, the hassle-free way to send WhatsApp messages without having to save contact information. With SendAWA, you no longer need to go through the tedious process of manually adding contacts to your phone before sending a message. All you have to do is enter the phone number, and SendAWA will create a WhatsApp conversation with the recipient instantly.

Our service uses official WhatsApp APIs, which means your chat is created through WhatsApp's secure servers. This ensures that your messages are delivered safely and securely to the recipient. Additionally, we do not store any personal information or the number provided on our servers, so you can trust that your data is always safe with us.

At sendawhats.app, we understand that privacy is important, and we strive to provide a service that respects your privacy. That's why we don't require you to sign up or provide any personal information. We also do not create tracking cookies. We simply provide a quick and easy way to send WhatsApp messages without the hassle of adding contacts.

Our service is perfect for those who need to send WhatsApp messages to new contacts quickly or those who want to keep their contact list clean and organized. No more adding weird nicknames or numbers to your contact list which you don't recognize later on. It's also great for businesses who want to send messages to customers without having to add them as contacts. If you contact a lot of people on WhatsApp, this is the perfect service for you.

We also detect your device's location and automatically add the country code to the number, so you don't have to worry about entering it manually. This makes it even easier to send messages to people in other countries.

Try our service today and experience the convenience of sending WhatsApp messages without the hassle. It's fast, easy, and secure. Just enter the phone number, and you're ready to go!
